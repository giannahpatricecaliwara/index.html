About Us



Donuts is one of the top merienda choices for Filipinos. Hailing from Davao, P. Donuts serves freshly-baked donuts. This Filipino-owned donut chain recently expanded from Mindanao to Luzon with relatively new branches in Malate, Manila and Cubao, Quezon City to allow more Filipinos to enjoy their novelty donuts.
